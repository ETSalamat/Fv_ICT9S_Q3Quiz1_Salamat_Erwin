"Spider-Man" focuses on the story of student Peter Parker, who gets bitten by a genetically-altered spider and gains super strength and spider-like abilities. He uses his powers to fight crime but he faces consequences and sacrifices along the way.

Spider-Man
Director: Sam Raimi

Casts: Tobey Maguire, Kirsten Dunst, Willem Dafoe, James Franco

Genre: Action, Superhero

Runtime: 121 Minutes

After a failed experiment, Dr. Otto Octavius loses control of the metal tentacles attached to his body and becomes Dr. Octopus. Spider-Man is blamed for the failure and Dr. Octopus claims to get his revenge. Meanwhile, Peter Parker deals with his life struggles and doubt, which started making his powers fade away.

While Peter Parker and MJ's relationship starts to gets better, the alien symbiote, Venom, takes over Peter's life. The Spider-Man suit turns black and he gains enhanced strength, but it also brings out the dark side of his personality. His only choice is to remove and destroy it while he also deals with two villains.
